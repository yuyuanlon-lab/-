Revitalization of a cowshed

Competition 2023, Finalist

Yangjiatang Village is a terraced mountain village. The buildings are built on the mountains, featuring traditional Chinese roofs and gable walls. The village can be seen in its entirety from the site, with its layers of grey tiles and brown walls. The abandoned cowshed is on the side of the village road leading to the hill, in front of the building is a small tea field, while on the hill behind it is the large tea fields of the village. The materials used for the cowsheds are highly regional, including tiles, stones, wood, earth.

The prototypical architectural elements of the dwellings were extracted from the village landscape and combined with the traditional materials used in the buildings, wooden roofs and rammed earth walls became the main architectural forms. To break up the originally closed cowshed, an open pavilion was attached to the building.
